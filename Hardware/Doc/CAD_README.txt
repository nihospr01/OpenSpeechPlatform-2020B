Modeled in Solidworks; the Zip files contained utilized “pack and go” from within the software. Extract files into target location and open top level assembly:
-MEZ_Case_V6
-AJB_Assembly

Parts were 3d printed on a Form2 SLA printer on 0.5mm or 0.25mm layer resolution with default orientation and support density. Jellybean parts require 150 micron and higher resolution printers however mezzanine cases can be printed at much lower resolutions and even on commercial FDM printers.
Analog Jellybeans utilize Polytek PT Flex 60 castable polyurethane rubber to create custom strain relief. The molds for this are contained in the top level Jellybean assembly and were printed using Form2 Printer.
