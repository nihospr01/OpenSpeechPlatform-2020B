Detailed Description

The Ten Band Filter bank is a 10 Band Multirate Filter Bank with its center frequencies located at 250Hz, 500Hz, 750Hz, 1kHz, 1.5kHz, 2kHz, 3kHz, 4kHz, 6kHz, 8kHz.
